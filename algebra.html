What is Algebra?

Algebra uses letters and symbols to represent numbers. It helps us solve equations and understand mathematical relationships.

Worked Example

Solve:

2x + 3 = 11

Subtract 3 from both sides:

2x = 8

Divide by 2:

x = 4
